How To Play

✧ 5 words are hidden within the 20 by 10 letter grid
✧ once you've found a word, enter the coordinates into the corresponding box
✧ the first box will ask for the starting row number (the list of numbers to thte lef the grid)
✧ the second box will ask for the starting column number (the list of numbers above of the grid)
✧ the next two boxes will ask for the ending row and column of the word
✧ the words can be horizontal, vertical, or diagonal

happy searching!
