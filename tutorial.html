How To Play

5 words are hidden within the 20 by 10 letter grid. once you've found a word, enter the coordinates into the corresponding box. the first box will ask for the starting column number (the list of numbers above the grid) and the second box will ask for the starting row number (the list of numbers to the left of the grid). the next two boxes will ask for the ending coordinates of the word. if you forget how to play, you can always click on the question mark in the top right corner to come back to this page
the words can be horizontal, vertical, or diagonal
happy searching!
